MafiaPS

Join a universe of limitless worlds, all interconnected. Build incredible worlds with friends and fill them with anything your heart desires! 

Collecting seeds, growing trees, and combining seeds to create new ones are at the core of the game. MafiaPS is filled with everyday heroes—and a few villains too!

Features 

  • Unlimited worlds 
  • Free to play, a single currency can be earned 
  • Full chat and messaging 
  • Thousands of amazing items to discover 
  • Plays great on big screen

A Creative Platformer

MafiaPS started with growing trees at its core, but over its 1-year history, it has blossomed into so much more. Become a surgeon and save lives, hone your skills as a top chef and create delicious meals, cast your fishing rod and land legendary catches, build ships and embark on star voyages, and craft Parkour worlds filled with mazes, traps, and puzzles. With endless possibilities and a thriving community, it's like having hundreds of games in one. Who will you be today?
